Campus Critters Chronicle

We personify animals instantly. We name them, talk to them, some even hangout with them as though they were friends. Once you start to engage with them regularly, they seem more and more human. With Campus Critters Chronicle, we want to document such intriguing interactions with our community animals. If you have any such stories to share, you can too submit them in the google form.
Hopefully this chronicle will make us reflect and make better the relationship we share with animals.
